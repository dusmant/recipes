This recipe talks about how to prepare mouth watering delicious chicken fry in cream

1. Take two pieces of chicken breast (skinned) around 400 to 500gm. Cut them into medicum size cubes, with thickness being around 1 to 1.5cm
2. Marinate those pieces in 150gm of cream, plus ginger and garlic paste, salt and little bit of lemon juice. Keep that marinated for 1 to 1.5 hours
3. Then heat the pan and fry it for just 3 to 5 minutes till the chicken is properly cooked and started turning slightly brown.
4. Serve it with garlic bread...
